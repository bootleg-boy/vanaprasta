About Our Mission

Creating Forests, Building Futures

The main aim of Vanaprastha Foundation is to make a "Vanaprastha Ashram". But very firstly the founders realized that if there is no Vana (forest) then how will be Vanaprastha.

That's why the TEAM decided to make Vana and started plantation since 2017. We are committed to environmental conservation and creating sustainable ecosystems for future generations.
